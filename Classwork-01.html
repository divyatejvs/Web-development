London is the capital of England. It is one of the most expensive cities in the world. This city lights up the most in the night under the night sky

London stands on the River Thames. On this river banks lies gaint wheel know to us as London Eye, or the Millenium wheel. It is Europe's tallest cantilevered observation wheel and is the most popular paid tourist attraction in the United Kingdom.
