Mona Lisa

Mona Lisa or Monna lisa is a half painting by infamous artist Leonardo da vinci. The painting is probably of the Italian noblewomen Lisa Gherardini, the wife of Francesco Del Giocondo. Mona Lisa is one of the most expensive paintings out there. It holds the Guinness World Record for the highest known painting insurance valuation at stagering US$100 Million in 1962. It may now worth around $870 Million.
